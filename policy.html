NumisGallery Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 17, 2026
NumisGallery is provided for educational and numismatic study. This online document covers privacy handling, compliance controls, and legal-use disclaimer.

1. Purpose and Scope

  • NumisGallery is an in-app study viewer for banknote-related educational and numismatic reference use.
  • NumisGallery is intended to showcase the beauty of banknotes, support appreciation, and help users revisit related historical memory and context.
  • NumisGallery is not a reproduction, transfer, or counterfeit-enabling tool.

2. Data and Content Handling

  • NumisGallery is designed for in-app educational viewing without account-based usage requirements.
  • The app does not include any third-party SDKs and serves as a focused, ad-free currency appreciation experience.
  • The app does not track user personal information for profiling or behavioral analytics.
  • Viewing-related app data is handled locally on-device, and the app does not provide built-in external sharing workflows.
  • My Collection is a paid, local-only feature. User-imported banknote images, notes, and related collection metadata are stored in app-managed local storage on the device.
  • NumisGallery does not provide account-based collection sync in the current version, and My Collection data is not automatically synchronized across devices by the app.
  • Local OCR may be used to help users fill in collection information. OCR results are assistive and can be reviewed or edited by the user.
  • Reference lookup, when used, is limited to text-based queries such as user-entered or locally recognized keywords. User-imported banknote images are not uploaded for remote image analysis.
  • Reference content is presented under an educational-use policy framework.

3. Safety and Compliance Framework

Built-in Safety & Boundary Controls
  • Compliance-first onboarding: users are clearly informed of educational-use boundaries before study flow begins.
  • Controlled study presentation: viewing behavior is intentionally constrained to support lawful educational reference use.
  • Output limitation: the product scope excludes built-in outward sharing/reproduction workflows.
  • Protective safeguards: the app applies compliance-oriented protective handling during usage scenarios that require caution.
  • Scope governance: content availability is managed through explicit release-scope and policy controls.
  • Takedown channel: rights-holders can request correction/removal via the support contact path.
  • Single-side display behavior is maintained in study flow.
  • Presentation constraints are applied to reduce confusion with real-world monetary use.
  • The app does not provide built-in export, share, or print workflows for banknote imagery.
  • Private collection features do not change the app's educational-use boundary, output limitations, or presentation constraints.
  • Additional protective handling is applied in sensitive usage scenarios.

4. Disclaimer and Legal Responsibility

  • All banknote imagery and related reference information are provided for educational and numismatic study.
  • Each banknote image includes source attribution information with direct links to the original source context.
  • Rights and restrictions, where applicable, remain with their respective owners, issuers, or licensors.
  • NumisGallery does not provide legal advice.
  • Users are solely responsible for complying with applicable laws, issuer rules, and local regulations in their jurisdiction.
  • Any use for unlawful reproduction, counterfeit-related activity, or rights infringement is outside intended use and not authorized by this app.

5. Takedown and Correction Requests

  • If you believe content infringes your rights or requires correction/removal, contact us with country code, denomination, and side (obverse/reverse).
  • Requests are reviewed and processed for update/removal where appropriate.
