NumisGallery Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 18, 2026
NumisGallery is an educational and numismatic study app. This policy explains what data is processed, how it is handled, and how compliance controls are enforced in product behavior.

1. Data Handling Scope

  • NumisGallery does not require account registration or user login.
  • Core browsing state, selected country/denomination, and local app data are stored on-device.
  • The app is designed for in-app study viewing, not for document reproduction workflows.

2. Network Use

  • The release app primarily uses bundled assets and seeded metadata.
  • Some image metadata includes Wikimedia source references; runtime fetch logic exists for controlled scenarios.
  • No in-app advertising SDKs are integrated in this project codebase.

3. Compliance Measures

App-Specific Controls
  • Single-side presentation: banknotes are displayed one side at a time.
  • Non-1:1 rendering: presentation constraints are applied to avoid print-like scale parity.
  • No export/share/print pipeline: the app does not provide built-in export, sharing, or printing.
  • Capture-awareness overlay: when system capture is detected, watermark protection is applied in gallery views.
  • Startup compliance gate: users must acknowledge the compliance notice before first hero reveal.
  • Release scope controls: market/currency availability is explicitly constrained by configuration, seed, and compliance matrix.

4. Disclaimer

  • NumisGallery is provided for educational and numismatic study only.
  • The app does not provide legal advice.
  • Users are responsible for complying with applicable laws, issuer rules, and local regulations in their jurisdiction.
  • Any misuse for reproduction, counterfeiting, or unlawful activity is strictly outside intended use.

5. Third-Party Content Attribution

  • Banknote images and related metadata may originate from third-party public sources (for example Wikimedia and issuer references).
  • Usage posture is tracked through project compliance governance and scoped release datasets.

6. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated as features, legal posture, or release scope evolves. The effective date at the top indicates the current version.
